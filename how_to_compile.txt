Jak to skompilowac na Linuxsie
gcc `pkg-config --cflags gtk+-3.0` -o gui gui.c `pkg-config --libs gtk+-3.0`


Jak to skompilowac na linuxsie do exe (format na Windowsa)

i686-w64-mingw32-gcc hello.c -o hello32.exe      # 32-bit
x86_64-w64-mingw32-gcc hello.c -o hello64.exe    # 64-bit


x86_64-w64-mingw32-gcc `pkg-config --cflags gtk+-3.0` -o gui.exe gui.c `pkg-config --libs gtk+-3.0`
